Explore Photoshop

About the Course

This Adobe Photoshop training course is designed to provide learners with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in image editing, graphic design, and digital art creation. The course is ideal for aspiring graphic designers, photographers, and creative professionals who want to master Photoshop's tools and techniques.

Key Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, participants will be able to:

  1. Perform professional-grade photo editing and retouching.
  2. Create visually appealing designs for digital and print media.
  3. Manipulate images and create composites with precision.
  4. Work efficiently using Photoshop's advanced tools and techniques.
  • Overview of Photoshop and its applications
  • Understanding the workspace: menus, panels, and toolbars
  • Image resolution, file types, and color modes (RGB, CMYK, etc.)
  • Setting up and saving projects
  • Selection Tools:
    • Marquee, Lasso, and Magic Wand
    • Quick selection and refine edge
  • Layers and Layer Management:
    • Creating, duplicating, and organizing layers
    • Layer masks and clipping masks
  • Transformations:
    • Scaling, rotating, skewing, and warping objects
    • Perspective corrections
  • Adjusting brightness, contrast, and color balance
  • Working with adjustment layers (Levels, Curves, Hue/Saturation)
  • Removing blemishes, wrinkles, and unwanted elements using:
    • Clone Stamp
    • Healing Brush
    • Patch Tool
    • Content-Aware Fill
  • Adding text and styling with layer effects
  • Applying gradients, patterns, and textures
  • Using filters and effects for artistic creations
  • Creating custom brushes and shapes
  • Composite creation and blending images
  • Advanced masking techniques
  • Smart Objects and non-destructive editing
  • Using Liquify for reshaping and artistic effects
  • Design Projects:
    • Social media post design
    • Poster and banner creation
    • Photo retouching and creative manipulation
  • Portfolio Building:
    • Organizing completed projects for presentation
    • Preparing print-ready and web-friendly files
  • 2–3 months (customizable)
  • Weekly sessions: 2–3 classes (2–3 hours per class)
