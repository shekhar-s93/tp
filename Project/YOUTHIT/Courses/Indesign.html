Explore InDesign

This Adobe InDesign training course is designed to provide learners with professional skills in page layout and design, focusing on creating stunning print and digital publications. It is ideal for graphic designers, publishers, and marketing professionals who aim to master InDesign for magazines, brochures, eBooks, and more.

Key Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, participants will be able to:

  1. Create professional print and digital layouts.
  2. Master advanced text and typography techniques.
  3. Design visually appealing multi-page documents.
  4. Prepare designs for publication in both print and digital formats.
  • Overview of InDesign and its applications
  • Navigating the workspace: tools, menus, and panels
  • Creating and saving documents
  • Understanding color modes, bleed, and slug
  • Creating custom document sizes
  • Setting up master pages and grids
  • Working with margins, columns, and rulers
  • Adding and managing pages
  • Adding and formatting text
  • Using character and paragraph styles
  • Text wrapping and flow
  • Working with special characters and glyphs
  • Placing and resizing images
  • Using the Frame tool for placeholders
  • Linking and embedding graphics
  • Image adjustments and fitting techniques
  • Creating multi-page layouts: brochures, magazines
  • Working with layers for complex designs
  • Adding interactive elements: buttons, hyperlinks, videos
  • Applying effects like drop shadows and gradients
  • Preparing files for print: preflight checks and packaging
  • Exporting to print-ready PDF with proper settings
  • Creating interactive PDFs and eBooks
  • Understanding file formats for digital platforms
  • Design Projects:
    • Brochures, flyers, and posters
    • Magazine layouts
    • Interactive eBooks or digital presentations
  • Portfolio Building:
    • Organizing and presenting completed projects
    • Preparing final files for both print and web
  • 2–3 months (customizable)
  • Weekly sessions: 2–3 classes (2–3 hours per class)
